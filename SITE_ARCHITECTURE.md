# Sonnie Studio Website Architecture

## 1. Project Goal

Build a studio website inspired by the conversion logic of `https://sc-icg.com/`, but repositioned around:

- AI Coding
- Fast launch
- Business-focused execution
- Small team / studio trust building

This should not look like a copy. We borrow the "sales structure" rather than the visual identity.

## 2. What The Reference Site Does Well

The reference site is effective because its homepage follows a clear conversion sequence:

1. Strong positioning headline
2. Trust-building capability statements
3. Portfolio / case proof
4. Service explanation
5. Why choose us
6. Contact / quote form

Its real strength is not the design alone. It is the way it removes risk for the client.

## 3. Recommended Positioning For Sonnie Studio

Suggested positioning:

`Sonnie Studio 是一間以 AI Coding 為核心的網站與產品工作室，幫品牌與創業者更快把想法變成可以上線、可以成交、可以持續優化的網站與工具。`

Three core value pillars:

- Faster than traditional agencies
- More flexible than template shops
- More business-aware than pure freelancers

## 4. Ideal Homepage Structure

### A. Hero

Purpose:
- Say what you do
- Say who you help
- Say why you are different

Suggested content:

- Main headline:
  `用 AI Coding，幫你更快做出真正能上線的網站`
- Subheadline:
  `Sonnie Studio 專注品牌網站、展示型網站與輕量商業工具開發，結合 AI 加速流程，但保留策略、設計與落地品質。`
- Primary CTA:
  `預約聊聊`
- Secondary CTA:
  `看案例`

### B. Why AI Coding

Purpose:
- Turn "AI" from gimmick into a business advantage

Suggested points:

- 需求整理更快
- 原型與頁面迭代更快
- 開發效率更高
- 上線與後續優化速度更快
- 成本更可控

Important:
Do not sell "AI" alone. Sell "AI + judgment + delivery".

### C. Services

Recommended 3-service model for MVP:

1. 品牌形象網站
2. 一頁式活動 / 產品頁
3. AI 驅動的小型工具 / 自動化頁面

Optional expanded items later:

- 網站改版
- 內容結構規劃
- SEO 基礎建置
- 維運與迭代合作

### D. Case Study Section

Because you currently have only one example, do not pretend to have many.

Recommended strategy:

- Put one real featured case front and center
- Write it deeply
- Show process, problem, solution, result

Case card structure:

- Client / project name
- Industry
- What the client needed
- What you built
- What changed after launch
- Tech / scope tags

This is stronger than showing fake quantity.

### E. Process

This is very important when you have limited portfolio depth.

Suggested 5-step process:

1. 初談與需求梳理
2. 結構與內容規劃
3. 視覺與頁面原型
4. AI Coding 開發與調整
5. 上線與後續優化

This section makes you feel reliable and organized.

### F. Why Choose Us

Suggested trust points:

- AI 加速，但不是套模板交差
- 站在商業目標思考，不只做漂亮畫面
- 能快速迭代，不會讓專案卡太久
- 溝通直接，適合需要高效率合作的客戶
- 可從網站延伸到自動化、小工具、後台流程

### G. FAQ

Very useful for conversion, especially for a newer studio.

Suggested FAQ:

- AI Coding 做的網站會不會很像模板？
- 你們適合哪一類型的客戶？
- 只有一頁式也可以合作嗎？
- 沒有完整文案也能開始嗎？
- 後續維護是否提供？

### H. Contact / Inquiry

For MVP, keep it simple.

Form fields:

- 姓名
- 品牌 / 公司名稱
- 聯絡方式
- 預算區間
- 想做的類型
- 專案簡述
- 參考網站

Primary job:
- Explain who you are

### Services

- Let visitors quickly understand what they can buy from you

Suggested structure:

### Case Study

Primary job:
- Prove execution ability

- Challenge
- Approach
- Design / build highlights
- Result
- Screenshots

### About

Primary job:
- Humanize the studio
- Explain your working philosophy

Include:

- Why Sonnie Studio exists
- Why AI Coding matters to your workflow

### Contact

Primary job:
- Convert serious leads

## 7. Design Direction Recommendation

To avoid looking like a generic agency clone:

- Use a cleaner, more product-studio style
- Fewer decorative elements than the reference site
- More editorial typography
- Strong contrast between strategy and build sections
- Clear, modern visual cues around speed and systems

Tone suggestion:

- Professional
- Sharp
- Calm confidence
- Tech-forward but not cold

## 8. Messaging Recommendation

Avoid vague lines like:

- 我們提供最好的服務
- 幫您打造專屬網站

Prefer sharper messaging like:

- 不是只把網站做出來，而是幫你把上線速度與商業表現一起拉起來
- 用 AI Coding 縮短開發時間，把更多力氣放在真正有價值的決策
- 從品牌展示到輕量工具，我們做的是能投入營運的數位資產

## 9. How To Handle Having Only One Case

This is the most important content strategy question right now.

Recommended approach:

- 1 real case study
- 3 service scenario cards
- 1 clear process section
- 1 founder / studio philosophy section
- 1 FAQ section

This combination can still create enough trust for early-stage sales.

Do not:

- Fake multiple cases
- Overstate team size
- Use exaggerated claims without proof
